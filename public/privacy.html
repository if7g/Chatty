This Privacy Policy describes how Chatty ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our Service. By using Chatty, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We collect the following types of information:

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Chat messages you send are transmitted to the NVIDIA AI API to generate responses. This means your prompts are processed by NVIDIA's infrastructure. We encourage you to review NVIDIA's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data. We do not control NVIDIA's data practices and are not responsible for them.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data and chat history for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you wish to delete your account or your data, please contact us through the Service. We will make reasonable efforts to remove your data upon request.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

Chatty may use session cookies or local storage to maintain your login session and preferences. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any analytics platforms that profile your behavior across other websites.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including hashing passwords before storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.
